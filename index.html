Benefits

Learn

Practice your skills with others learning with you. Give and recieve advice with your peers in a casual learning enviornment.

Contributing

Members can vollunteer or be hired to make artistic assets for the community. Such as posters for an event or student art on campus.

Fun

Making art during lunch is obviously very fun. It's possibly one of the most chill clubs at the school. Whether you come to every meeting, or you just wanna check it out once, it's only as high of a commitment as you want it to be.

Mission statement

ODAC strives to make a casual and creative learning environment for students who want to practice their skills at their own pace. We want members to communicate, critique, and encourage other artists in the club, in order to imprive their skillset.
